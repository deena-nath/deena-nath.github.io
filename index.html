Who Am I?

Hi! my name is Deena Nath, and I am a Ph.D Research Scholar at Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar University, Lucknow. I’ve worked as Assistant Professor at Babu Banarasi Das Institute of Technology and Management, Lucknow. I have 6 years of experience working with educational institutes. I have a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering. I consider myself a good public speaker, and I have a good sense of humor. I have an ability to plan, schedule, and handle many different tasks at once. This flexibility helps me in the classroom, where there are many different personalities and learning styles.

I enjoy programming, and the knowledge and perspective that my programming has strengthened my teaching skills and presentation abilities.

Since I am good at programming, I am a part-time website developer too. I also make portrait and observe people. Apart form this, I like writing quotes and short stories.
